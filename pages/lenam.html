Musanga Organization:

L'ENAM

Project

Projet artistique communautaire de Libreville: L’ENAM will occur from mid March through early June. We propose to host a series of 12 meetings: 7 meetings for artist workshops, exhibition planning and community programming amongst Gabonese, French and expat artists; and 5 meetings for a final exhibition, Environnement: Je suis là, all occurring on the campus of L’ENAM in building X.

Participants will be drawn from the L’ENAM artistic community, the French community, and the greater expat community. The 7 initial meetings will run from mid March through late May, with a break in April. The final exhibition, Environnement: Je suis là, will occur over the course of 5 final meeting dates, June 7th-11th. It will showcase the fruits of our artistic collaboration and community programming events. The exhibition will address the theme of environment in its multiplicity of meanings–as the space which surrounds us, the place in which we live, our neighborhood, and the land that we must care for. A schedule detailing the goals for each meeting and the exhibition timeline, subject to small revisions, is attached.

The initial 7 meetings will offer extensive opportunities for creative sharing and networking amongst the cohort. During the initial meeting we will introduce the project and participating artists will share their current work and talk about their practice. During the following meetings we will have conversations about in-process work, host 3 community programming events and plan for the exhibition. During this time the interior of the building will be cleaned and selectively painted white, a small road will be built, a small café will be built, and other necessary improvements will be made in anticipation of the exhibition events–-all with the permission of L’ENAM.

The final exhibition, entitled Environnement: Je suis là, will be curated by Sara Dolatabadi and Aschely Cone. It will showcase the fruits of our artistic collaboration and cross-cultural sharing. The exhibition will also include the work of other guest participants, artworks from community programming events, and a projection displaying images and videos documenting the Project as a whole. The exhibition will address the theme of environment in its multiplicity of meanings–-as the amorphous space in which we exist, as the place in which we live, as our neighborhood, and as the land for which we must care.

Our final exhibition, with which the project will culminate, will offer a final opportunity for cross-cultural sharing and networking. There will be two openings: one on Saturday, open to all, during which we will especially seek to engage the surrounding neighborhood and the artistic community of L’ENAM; the other on Friday, focused on engaging Gabonese government officials, businessmen and the diplomatic corps. We will do thorough and comprehensive publicity outreach using social media (Instagram and Facebook), print media and a comprehensive press package.

Objectifs de programmation

  • Offrir des opportunités de création artistique, de collaboration interculturelle et de mise en réseau entre les artistes gabonais, français et expatriés.
  • Proposer 7 rencontres pour des ateliers d'artistes, la planification d'expositions et des événements de programmation communautaire, offrant des opportunités de collaboration interculturelle.
  • Organiser une exposition finale au cours des 5 réunions finales.
  • Organiser deux vernissages à large diffusion le 9 et 10 juin (vendredi et samedi soir) pour notre exposition Environnement: Je suis là. Les deux événements compteront avec des stands de nourriture locale, de la musique locale en direct et une programmation supplémentaire pour apporter de l'énergie et de l'enthousiasme aux événements d'ouverture.
  • A long terme, nous espérons que nos événements serviront à développer d’autres programmes et opportunités d'exposition et de reconnaissance pour les artistes gabonais. Nous espérons également que notre programme offrira la possibilité d’organiser une exposition à L'ENAM, et qu'il renforcera les relations et le partage entre les artistes gabonais, français et expatriés.

Public cible

  • Les artistes gabonais locaux et les membres artistiques de la communauté des expatriés seront le public cible du projet dans son ensemble.
  • Les familles du quartier de L'ENAM et les personnes intéressées à Libreville seront le public cible des ateliers ouverts.
  • Les enfants de moins de 13 ans seront le public cible de nos événements supplémentaires de programmation communautaire.
  • La communauté du grand Libreville sera le public cible de notre événement d'ouverture de l'exposition finale.
  • Les fonctionnaires du gouvernement gabonais, les hommes d'affaires et le corps diplomatique seront le public cible de notre événement d'ouverture de l'exposition initiale.
